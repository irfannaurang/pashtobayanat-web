Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I download lectures for offline listening?

A: Open any lecture, tap the download icon, and wait for the download to complete. The lecture will be available offline in your library.

Q: Can I sync my bookmarks between devices?

A: Currently, bookmarks are stored locally on your device. To sync across devices, use the optional Navidrome server integration in Settings.

Q: How do I change the playback speed?

A: During playback, tap the speed button and select your preferred speed (0.5x to 2.0x).

Q: What should I do if the app crashes?

A:
1. Force close the app
2. Restart your device
3. Update to the latest iOS version
4. Reinstall the app if issues persist
5. Email us with details if problems continue

Q: How do I use Navidrome integration?

A:
1. Have a running Navidrome server with HTTPS enabled
2. Open App Settings
3. Enter your server URL and credentials
4. The app will connect to your personal server

Q: Can I restore my bookmarks after uninstalling?

A: If you enabled iCloud sync (if available), bookmarks may sync. Otherwise, bookmarks are lost when you uninstall. We recommend exporting your bookmarks before uninstalling.

Q: How do I report a bug?

A: Email us with:
1. Detailed description of the issue
2. Steps to reproduce it
3. Your device model and iOS version
4. App version (found in Settings → About)

Q: Is there a minimum iOS version required?

A: Yes, the app requires iOS 16.0 or later. Check the App Store for the latest requirements.

Q: Can I use the app without an internet connection?

A: Yes! Download lectures first, then listen offline. Internet is only needed for streaming or initial downloads.
