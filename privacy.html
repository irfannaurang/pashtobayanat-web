Privacy Policy - Pashto Bayanat

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Overview

Pashto Bayanat ("App") is a mobile application for listening to and managing Pashto audio lectures. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Key Point: Pashto Bayanat does NOT collect, track, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All your data remains on your device.

2. What Data the App Collects Locally

All of the following information is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Bookmarked Lectures: Titles and metadata of lectures you bookmark
  • Downloaded Audio Files: Audio files you download for offline listening
  • Playback Progress: Your listening position in lectures (automatically saved)
  • App Settings: Your preferences for playback speed, language, and theme
  • Playback History: Currently playing lecture information (used for lock screen display)

3. How Your Data is Stored

  • All settings and bookmarks are encrypted and stored in iOS UserDefaults on your device
  • Downloaded audio files are stored in your device's local storage (Documents folder)
  • No data is synced to cloud servers (iCloud, our servers, or any third parties)
  • If you uninstall the App, all local data is removed from your device

4. What We Do NOT Do

  • No Tracking: We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or tracking SDKs
  • No Advertising: The App contains no advertisements or ad networks
  • No Data Sharing: We never sell, share, or transmit your data to third parties
  • No Cloud Sync: Your bookmarks and settings are not backed up to iCloud or our servers
  • No Remote Servers: Except for optional features (see below), the App does not communicate with any remote servers

5. Audio Streaming & Playback

The App supports two playback modes:

Mode 1: Local Playback (Default)

  • You download audio files to your device
  • Files are stored locally and played locally
  • No internet connection required after download
  • Files remain on your device until you delete them

Mode 2: Streaming from Public URLs

  • Audio streams directly from public sources to your device
  • We do not store streaming URLs on your device after you close the App
  • Internet connection required for streaming

6. Optional: Navidrome Server Integration

If you choose to connect the App to your own Navidrome server:

  • Your Choice: This feature is optional and requires your action to configure
  • Credentials: Your username and password are stored securely on your device only
  • Server Communication: Audio streams directly between your device and your personal Navidrome server
  • Privacy: We never see your Navidrome credentials or any data transmitted to your server
  • Security: Must use HTTPS with TLS 1.2+ encryption for secure connections

7. Technical Information Collected

The App collects minimal technical information for stability:

  • App crash logs (stored locally, not transmitted)
  • iOS version (for compatibility)
  • App version (for updates)

This information is never sent to our servers.

8. Permissions Used

The App requests the following device permissions:

  • Audio Playback: Required to play audio files
  • File Storage: Required to download and store audio files
  • Network: Required to stream audio and check for app updates
  • MediaPlayer: Required to display information on lock screen

These permissions are used only for their stated purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

Pashto Bayanat is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has used the App, we will delete any associated data.

10. Data Retention

  • Downloaded Files: Retained until you manually delete them
  • Bookmarks: Retained until you remove them
  • Settings: Retained while the App is installed
  • All Data: Automatically deleted when you uninstall the App

11. Security Measures

  • All data stored on your device uses iOS encryption
  • Network connections use TLS 1.2+ encryption
  • Credentials are stored in encrypted iOS Keychain (for Navidrome integration)
  • We do not store sensitive information in plaintext

12. Third-Party Services

The App uses only standard Apple frameworks:

  • AVFoundation (audio playback)
  • MediaPlayer (lock screen controls)
  • Foundation (core functionality)
  • URLSession (network requests)

None of these frameworks transmit your personal data.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
